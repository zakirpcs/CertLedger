How it works
1
Generate a CSR

Create a Certificate Signing Request (CSR) using OpenSSL or your server's tools.

openssl req -new -newkey rsa:2048 \
  -keyout server.key -out server.csr
2
Submit for Signing

Upload your CSR file with your contact information. The CA admin will review your request.

Go to Submit
3
Download Certificate

Once approved, download your signed certificate and install it on your server.
